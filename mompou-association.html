Our Foundation

Two Main Pillars of Our Mission

Everything we do is built on these core commitments to our community's growth, resilience, and prosperity.

Social Welfare

We invest in the next generation through organised sports programmes, skills workshops, leadership training, and recreational activities that build character and teamwork among our youth.

Small_medium Enterprise (SME)

Association Grandstand & Office Project

The Mompou Association Grandstand & Office Project is one of our key community development initiatives aimed at strengthening both sporting infrastructure and administrative capacity within our association. This integrated project combines the construction of a modern grandstand and a functional office space to better serve our members and the wider community. The grandstand component provides a safe, organized, and comfortable viewing area for sports events, community gatherings, and cultural activities. It enhances the experience of players and supporters while promoting greater participation in local rugby league and other recreational programs. The office component serves as the operational hub of the association, where planning, coordination, and delivery of programs such as SME support, youth empowerment, education assistance, and social welfare services are managed. This ensures more efficient service delivery and improved communication within the association.

Islands Petroleum Fabrication Project

The Islands Petroleum Fabrication Project is a significant subcontract work completed by the Mompou Association’s Welding and Fabrication Team in partnership with Islands Petroleum. This project reflects the association’s growing capacity to deliver professional industrial services while creating real employment opportunities for local skilled workers. The main objective of the project was to carry out welding and fabrication works to support Islands Petroleum’s operational requirements. Our team was responsible for producing quality steel structures and fabrication components, ensuring that all work met required safety and industry standards. This project provided valuable hands-on experience for our members, allowing them to apply their technical skills in a real industrial environment. It also helped improve workmanship standards, teamwork, and workplace discipline, which are essential for future larger-scale contracts.

Active

Road Gravelling Project

The Mompou Association’s Road Gravelling Project is a key community infrastructure initiative aimed at improving access, connectivity, and transportation within our local area. This project involves the upgrading of existing dirt roads by spreading and compacting gravel to create a more durable and reliable road surface. The improvement of these roads plays an important role in supporting everyday community activities, including access to schools, health services, markets, and business operations. It also helps reduce transport difficulties during wet weather, making travel safer and more efficient for both pedestrians and vehicles. In addition to improving mobility, the project provides opportunities for local employment through the engagement of small contractors, machine operators, and laborers. This supports the association’s broader SME development goals by stimulating local economic activity and skills development.

Planning

Cultural Obligations Initiative

The Cultural Obligations Initiative is an important welfare and support program under the Mompou Association, designed to assist registered members in fulfilling key cultural and traditional responsibilities within the community. This initiative recognizes the importance of cultural practices such as bride price ceremonies, funeral contributions, and other customary obligations that are part of our social structure. The main aim of this program is to provide structured support to members during significant life events, ensuring that financial pressure does not prevent them from meeting their cultural responsibilities. Through this initiative, the association helps contribute to bride price ceremonies, funeral expenses, and other agreed cultural obligations in a fair and organized manner. This program is strictly available to registered members of the association, promoting accountability, unity, and shared responsibility among members. It also strengthens the bond between families and the association by ensuring that members can rely on collective support during important and often challenging times..

Active

Youth sports Program

The Mompou Association’s Youth Sports Program is a community-focused initiative designed to actively engage young people in organized sporting activities, with the aim of promoting healthy lifestyles, discipline, and positive personal development. A key objective of this program is to keep youth away from harmful influences such as drugs and alcohol by providing them with constructive alternatives that build confidence, teamwork, and leadership skills. Through regular training sessions, competitions, and community events, young people are encouraged to focus their energy on sport and personal growth. The program also aims to identify and nurture sporting talent, giving youth the opportunity to develop their skills and potentially progress into higher levels of competition in their chosen sporting fields. This creates pathways for future success, both on and off the field. Overall, the Youth Sports Program reflects the association’s commitment to youth empowerment, social well-being, and community development. By investing in our young people today, we are building a stronger, healthier, and more productive future for our community.

Planning

Community SME Start-up project Initiative

The Community SME Start-up Project Initiative is a key development program of the Mompou Association aimed at empowering local individuals and families to establish and grow small and medium enterprises within the community. The main goal of this initiative is to create sustainable income opportunities and reduce unemployment by supporting grassroots business development. Through this project, the association seeks to provide practical support such as basic business training, mentorship, access to small-scale funding opportunities, and guidance on business planning and management. The focus is on helping individuals turn their skills, ideas, and resources into viable income-generating activities. This initiative also aims to strengthen the local economy by encouraging entrepreneurship in areas such as trade, agriculture, services, and small-scale production. By supporting start-up businesses, the project helps build financial independence and reduces reliance on limited formal employment opportunities.
